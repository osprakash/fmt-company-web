## 1. Business Context

### 1.1 Purpose
The Customer Support Service provides ticket management, knowledge base, and customer communication capabilities for FlowMind products.

### 1.2 Scope
| In Scope | Out of Scope |
|----------|--------------|
| Ticket management | Live chat (integrate Intercom) |
| Knowledge base | Phone support |
| SLA tracking | |
| CSAT collection | |

---

## 2. Functional Requirements

### 2.1 Tickets
- Create, update, close tickets
- Priority levels (low, medium, high, urgent)
- Assignee management
- Ticket history/timeline

### 2.2 Knowledge Base
- Article management
- Categories and tags
- Search functionality
- Article analytics

### 2.3 SLA
- Response time tracking
- Resolution time tracking
- SLA breach alerts
